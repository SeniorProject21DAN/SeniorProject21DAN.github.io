Cast and Control

Vision

Cast and Control intends to research the possible applications of mobile phones as input devices. Challenges to be faced are the imprecision of the sensors, potentially slow reaction speeds from the phone, and inter-device network latency. Special attention will be paid towards gesture recognition from the sensors, as well as communication between either multiple mobile devices or the mobile device and a desktop device. The goal is to produce an intuitive API that developers can use to produce usable applications.

The objective of this software development project is to gain experience in three dimensional motion, improve our own ability to design and implement mobile applications, and attempt to build software capable of interfacing with different devices and operating systems. Additional research will be done to test the limitations of the suite of motion sensors available within smartphones.
